Frequently Asked Questions
How long does it take to process deposits?

Bank transfers are usually processed instantly. Card payments are processed immediately upon successful verification.

Are there any fees for deposits?

We don't charge any fees for deposits. However, your bank or card provider may charge their own transaction fees.

What is the minimum deposit amount?

The minimum deposit amount is ₦100. There's no maximum limit, but deposits over ₦1,000,000 may require additional verification.

Can I get a refund for my deposit?

Deposits can be withdrawn back to your bank account. Please contact support for withdrawal requests.
