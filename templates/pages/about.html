About OpenSell

Welcome to OpenSell MarketPlace – Nigeria's premier digital marketplace designed to transform how Nigerians buy and sell locally. We're not just another classifieds platform; we're revolutionizing peer-to-peer commerce across the nation.

Our Vision

At OpenSell, we envision a Nigeria where commerce is simplified, accessible, and truly empowering for all citizens. From urban centers to rural communities, we're building bridges that connect buyers and sellers efficiently and securely.

We're pioneering a movement toward a more collaborative economy where individuals can unlock the value of their assets and find exactly what they need—all within their local communities.

Our Story

Founded in Iwo, Osun State, OpenSell began with a simple observation: Nigerians needed a better way to connect and trade with confidence. What started as a local solution has quickly grown into one of Nigeria's most trusted marketplace platforms.

Our team combines deep expertise in technology with authentic Nigerian market insights, creating a platform that truly understands the unique needs of our users and communities.

Company Information

OpenSell MarketPlace operates as a registered legal entity under the name OPENSELL MARKETPLACE LIMITED, with Company Registration Number 8904110, duly registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of Nigeria.

Our registration reflects our commitment to operating transparently within the legal framework of Nigerian commerce, providing our users with the assurance of dealing with a properly constituted corporate entity.

The OpenSell Difference

Introductory No-Fee Period: To celebrate our launch, OpenSell is initially offering zero transaction fees for early adopters. This limited-time opportunity allows our pioneering users to experience our full platform capabilities while keeping 100% of their transactions.

Our Commitment to Safety

User safety is our paramount concern. We've implemented robust verification processes, secure messaging systems, and comprehensive educational resources to ensure safe transactions.

Our dedicated moderation team works tirelessly to review listings, verify accounts, and maintain the integrity of our marketplace environment.

Our Impact on Nigerian Commerce

OpenSell is emerging as an exciting new force in Nigeria's digital commerce landscape. As a fresh entrant to the market, we're already facilitating meaningful connections between buyers and sellers, contributing to the growth of local economies.

We're creating entrepreneurial opportunities for Nigerians from all walks of life, empowering individuals to start micro-businesses and generate income through our innovative marketplace platform.
