Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how OpenSell ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our marketplace platform. It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them. We encourage you to read this policy carefully to understand how we handle cookies and similar technologies.

What Are Cookies?

Definition: Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

Storage Duration: Cookies can remain on your device for different periods. Session cookies last only while your browser is open and are automatically deleted when you close it. Persistent cookies last until they expire or are deleted.

First and Third-Party Cookies: First-party cookies are set by the website you're visiting. Third-party cookies are set by a third party, such as an analytics service.

How We Use Cookies

Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, account authentication, and maintaining your preferences. You cannot opt out of these cookies.

Functional Cookies: These cookies help us to personalize and enhance your user experience. They may be set by us or by third parties whose services we have added to our pages. If you disable these cookies, some or all of these services may not function properly.

Performance and Analytics Cookies: These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources, measure and improve the performance of our site. They help us know which pages are popular and see how visitors move around the site. All information these cookies collect is aggregated.

Limited Purpose: In line with our Privacy Policy, we use cookies solely for enhancing user experience and ensuring platform functionality. We do not use cookies for marketing, advertising, or profiling purposes.

Specific Cookies We Use

Session Cookies: We use session cookies to keep you logged into our platform during your visit, remembering your preferences and selections as you navigate between pages.

Authentication Cookies: These cookies help us identify users who are logged into their accounts, allowing us to provide personalized features and secure access to user accounts.

CSRF Token Cookies: These cookies are essential security features that protect both our platform and your data from certain types of attacks by ensuring that form submissions come from legitimate users of our site.

Analytics Cookies: We use anonymous analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website, helping us improve the functionality and user experience of our platform.

Other Tracking Technologies

Web Beacons: We may use web beacons (small graphic images) to monitor the traffic patterns of users from one page to another and to improve site performance. Like cookies, these do not identify individual users.

Local Storage: We may use HTML5 local storage to enhance your experience on our platform. Local storage is similar to cookies but can store larger amounts of data.
