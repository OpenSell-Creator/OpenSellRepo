Safety Tips

Your safety is important to us. Please follow these tips when buying and selling on OpenSell to ensure a secure and positive experience.

Meet in public places

Always meet in well-lit, populated areas preferably during the day and in busy locations like shopping malls, cafes, or outside police stations.

Inspect before you buy

Test and verify the product thoroughly before making payment. Ensure that it matches the description and functions as expected.

Avoid upfront payments

Pay only when you've received and inspected the item. Be wary of requests for deposits or advance payments, especially through untraceable methods.

Take someone along

Consider bringing a friend or family member with you, especially if it's your first time meeting the buyer/seller or when dealing with high-value items.

Trust your instincts

If something feels wrong or too good to be true, it probably is. Don't be afraid to walk away from a deal that doesn't feel right.

Additional Safety Guidelines

  • Keep your personal information private until necessary this includes home address
  • Research the item's market value before buying or selling
  • Be cautious of offers that seem too good to be true
  • Document transactions with receipts when possible
  • Report suspicious activities to OpenSell immediately

Stay smart. Trade safe.

If you encounter any suspicious activity or have safety concerns, please contact us immediately.
