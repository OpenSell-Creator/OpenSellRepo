Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use OpenSell Market Place.

Location Information: We collect profile location data solely to provide location-based filtering of products to improve user experience. We do not use this data for any other purpose.

Listing Information: Product details, descriptions, and images you provide when posting an ad.

Usage Information: Data like your IP address or location may be collected to improve your experience.

Reporting Issues: If you experience any negative incidents or safety concerns while using our platform, please contact us immediately with relevant documentation or evidence. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information.
